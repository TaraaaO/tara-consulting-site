Meet Tara

I've worked across operations, training, compliance, marketing and business systems in real Aussie industries — from trades to tourism to early childhood education.

I started Tara Consulting Co because I kept seeing the same thing: hard-working business owners who are great at what they do, but stretched thin trying to keep up with social media, AI, marketing and admin all at once.

Most of what I help with doesn't require a big agency or a long complicated strategy. It just requires someone to sit down with you, figure out what actually matters, and help you build something simple and repeatable that you can keep using.

No jargon. No overcomplicating it. Just practical support that helps you move.

What I believe
  • Clear communication solves most problems. Most marketing issues are actually communication issues in disguise.
  • Simple and practical beats complicated every time. If you won't use it, it doesn't matter how good it looks.
  • Real-world experience matters as much as theory. I've worked in the industries I help, not just read about them.
  • Consistency is more valuable than perfection. Showing up imperfectly beats the perfect post you never make.
  • AI is a tool, not a shortcut. Used properly it saves real time. Used lazily it produces generic rubbish.
What I work across

My background spans training and assessment, operations management, social media, content creation and business systems. I work across a small number of industries where I have genuine hands-on experience:

  • Early childhood education and care — training, compliance, student support and centre operations
  • Tourism and hospitality — social media, content strategy and marketing for visitor-facing businesses
  • Trades and construction — operations, job management, admin systems and processes
  • Small business generally — wherever social media, AI, marketing or operations support is needed
