Privacy policy.

Your trust matters to me. This page explains, in plain language, what information I collect, why, and how I keep it safe.

Last updated: June 2026

Who I am

Tara Consulting Co is a marketing and business-systems consultancy based on the NSW South Coast, Australia, run by Tara Odgers. If you have any questions about your privacy or this policy, you can reach me any time at hello@taraconsultingco.com.

What I collect

I only collect the information I genuinely need to help you. Depending on how you interact with me, that may include:

  • Your name and email address, when you request a free resource, get in touch, or become a client.
  • Business details you choose to share with me, so I can provide relevant advice and support.
  • Information you enter into the client portal, if you become a client and I set up an account for you.
  • Basic, non-identifying information about how the website is used, which helps me improve it.

How I use it

I use your information to:

  • Send you the free resource or information you have asked for.
  • Respond to your enquiries and provide the services we have discussed.
  • Occasionally send you helpful updates, if you have opted in. You can unsubscribe at any time.
  • Manage your client account and deliver the work you have engaged me for.

I will never sell your information, and I will never share it with anyone else for their own marketing.

How I keep it safe

I take reasonable steps to protect your information. The website and client portal use secure, encrypted connections, and access to client information is restricted. The tools I use to run my business are reputable providers with their own strong security practices. While no system can ever be guaranteed completely secure, I treat your information with care and only keep it for as long as I need it.

Sharing with third parties

To run my business, I use trusted third-party services, for example to host the website, store files, and send emails. These providers only handle your information to provide their service to me, and are bound by their own privacy and security obligations. I do not give them permission to use your information for anything else.

Your choices

You are always in control of your information. You can:

  • Ask me what information I hold about you.
  • Ask me to correct or update it.
  • Ask me to delete it, where I am not required to keep it.
  • Unsubscribe from any updates at any time.

Just email me at hello@taraconsultingco.com and I will sort it out for you.

Cookies and the website

The website may use simple cookies or similar technology to help it work properly and to understand, in a general way, how it is being used. This does not personally identify you, and you can control cookies through your own browser settings.

Changes to this policy

If I update how I handle information, I will update this page and change the date at the top. I would encourage you to check back from time to time.

Questions

If anything here isn't clear, or you would like to talk through how your information is handled, please get in touch at hello@taraconsultingco.com. I'm always happy to help.
